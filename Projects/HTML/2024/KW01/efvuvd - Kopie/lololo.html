Vanillekipferl

These crumbly vanilla crescents are sure to be a very popular biscuit, not only at Christmas.

So why dont we make some!

Ingredients

Number of Persons
250g flour
100g ground peeled almonds
80g icing sugar
1 parcel vanilla sugar
1 pinch salt
200g butter, cold
1 Egg
6 TS icing sugar
1 parcel Vanilla sugar

Recipe

Step 1: Mix flour, almonds, powdered sugar, vanilla sugar and salt in a bowl.

Step 2: Cut the butter into pieces, add and rub by hand into a crumbly mixture.

Step 3: Beat the egg, add it to the mixture.

Step 4: Combine into a dough, flatten and cover and refrigerate for about 2 hours.

Step 5: Preheat oven to 160 degrees. Shape the dough into finger-thick rolls in portions, cut into pieces about. 2 cm long, twist into pencil-thick rolls, shape into crescents.

Step 6: Place on a baking tray lined with baking paper, refrigerate for about. 15 mins.

Step 7: Bake: about. 15 min. in the middle of the oven.

Step 8: Remove from the oven, allow to cool slightly.

Step 9: Mix powdered sugar and vanilla sugar, dust lukewarm vanilla crescents with it, carefully remove from the baking paper and leave to cool on a wire rack.

Step 10: Bon Apetit!
